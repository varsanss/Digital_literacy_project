Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

✅ DO's

Keep your LinkedIn and GitHub profiles updated and professional.
Use your college email for all academic and professional sign-ups.
Think before you post — ask yourself if you'd be comfortable with your professor seeing it.
Follow industry professionals and companies in your field to stay updated.
Use social media to showcase your academic projects, certifications, and achievements.

❌ DON'Ts

Don't share personal information like your address, phone number, or Aadhaar details publicly.
Don't post negative comments about your college, professors, or classmates online.
Don't share OTPs, passwords, or banking details with anyone — even in private messages.
Don't engage with or spread unverified news and forwards on WhatsApp or Twitter/X.
Don't use informal language, slang, or emojis when communicating with professors or recruiters online.
